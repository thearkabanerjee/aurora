What Aurora.AI does

  • Personalized Sky Tours

    Tell Aurora your location and mood — it narrates a 5-minute stargazing story built just for you.

  • Event Alerts

    Never miss a meteor shower or a close-approach — smart notifications tuned to your schedule.

  • Friendly Explanations

    Complex astronomy made human: why Jupiter looks bright, what a retrograde is, and why the Moon wobbles.

How it works

  1. Enter your location and preference.
  2. Aurora composes a short guided tour using curated astronomy facts.
  3. Receive audio/text tour and optional calendar reminders for events.
